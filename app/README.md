# StudySquared

An open-source study tool for students to take notes and more

## Features
- **Notes and notebooks**: Organize your study material with an intutive structure
- **Rich text editor**: Take notes and style them with rich text
- **OCR** *(coming soon)*: Convert your handwritten notes into digital notes
- **Quizzes and flashcards** *(coming soon)*: Test your knowledge with quizzes and flashcards
- **Collaboration** *(coming soon)*: Share notes with others and collaborate on study materials
- **Notes feed** *(coming soon)*: Publish your notes to share them with others
